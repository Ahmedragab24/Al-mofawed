Mission

AL Mufawed is a leading Financial Management and Claims Recovery Services company with a personal approach. We serve businesses and individuals in the pursuit of managing claims successfully, recovering monies swiftly and salvaging valuable business relationships.

Settlement of financial claims to help resolve the dispute in a manner agreed upon by the disputing parties. These companies provide legal and financial advice and guidance and negotiate on behalf of clients to reach a fair and satisfactory settlement agreement for all.

Financial claims adjusters work in a variety of areas, including:

  • Insurance: It deals with the settlement of insurance claims, whether they are of a personal nature, such as health and automobile insurance, or a commercial nature, such as property and liability insurance.
  • Financial Services: Helps settle claims related to financial services such as loans, mortgages, and credit cards.
  • Commercial contracts: settles financial claims between companies And individuals in relation to commercial contracts and engagements.

The company relies on a team of experts specialized in negotiation, financial and legal analysis. These companies may also use alternative means of dispute resolution such as arbitration or conciliation.

The mission of this company is to achieve a fair and satisfactory settlement for all, saving the time, effort and costs associated with lengthy court proceedings. Clients can benefit from the experience and knowledge of these companies to achieve a positive outcome in financial settlement cases.
